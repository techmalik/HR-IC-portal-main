# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Public auth pages that can appear in search or shared links (`/`, `/login`, `/signup`)
- Public SEO/content pages (`/blog`, `/blog/:slug`, `/faq`, `/industries`, `/compare`, programmatic landing pages)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated dashboard and app routes
- Admin and back-office routes
- API endpoints except where they generate public SEO assets such as `robots.txt` and sitemaps

## Target audience
- Operations leaders, founders, and admins who manage independent contractors
- Independent contractors interacting with time tracking, invoices, and leave workflows

## Primary keywords
- Contractor management software
- Contractor timesheets
- Contractor invoicing
- Contractor management for specific industries
- Alternatives/comparison keywords for competitor pages
