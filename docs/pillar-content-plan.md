# Axle 10-Pillar Content Plan

This is the editorial roadmap for Axle's organic search strategy. Each pillar
groups a high-intent commercial topic with a cluster of supporting articles,
programmatic SEO pages, and internal-link surfaces.

---

## Pillar 1 — Contractor Management Software (Category)

**Primary keyword:** contractor management software
**Search intent:** Commercial — buyer evaluating tools.
**Target page:** `/` (home) reinforced by `/blog/contractor-management-software`.

**Supporting cluster:**
- `/blog/best-contractor-management-software-2026`
- `/blog/contractor-management-vs-employee-management`
- `/blog/contractor-management-checklist`
- 20 industry pages at `/contractor-management-for-:industry`
- 20 comparison pages at `/:competitor-alternative`

**Internal links:** Every industry/competitor page links back to the home pillar.

## Cadence

- 2 long-form blog articles per week (one new, one refresh).
- 4 programmatic pages added per month (mix of industry + competitor).
- Quarterly internal-link audit so every blog cluster links back to its pillar
  and to relevant programmatic pages.
- Monthly review of GSC impressions/CTR per pillar; double down on top 2.

## KPIs

- Indexed programmatic pages / total programmatic pages (target 95%).
- Top-10 ranking keywords (target 100 within 6 months).
- Branded vs non-branded organic traffic split (target 60% non-branded).
- Signups attributed to organic (`/signup` referrer = blog or programmatic).
